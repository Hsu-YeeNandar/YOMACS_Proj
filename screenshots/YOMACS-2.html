Yoma Car Share Corporate Account

Business travel is easier and better with us. We provide effortless planning for business travel with our flexible rental period. You can rent from minimum 1-hour to maximum 30-days. Our rental hubs in Yangon and Mandalay are located at City Centers and we are also available in major airports in the country. Rent on the go with us to solve your on-demand corporate transportation needs. Reserve and pick-up the car at any rental hub near you anytime of the day and we also offer one-way drop option - that is you can pick-up from one rental hub and drop off at another hub.
